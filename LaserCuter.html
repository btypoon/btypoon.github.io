Laser Cutting intruction

Drawing notes

  • CoralDraw was used
  • The origin of the coordinate system of the laser cutter is located on the top left corner. While, the origin of the coordinate system of the CoralDraw software is located on the bottom left corner
  • Hairline identify cut line. All other line identify engraving line.
  • Go into the preference setting in the printing dialogue. Change the power and speed of different color. White color refers to emptyness. Usually, black color refers to cut line, other color refers to engraving area.
  • It is adviced to not change the power. Change the motor speed to adjust the engraving depth
  • For 3mm thickness board, using Power of 80 and Speed of 2.5 for cutting. Use power of 80 and soeed of 50 for engraving. Adjust the speed of engraving to suit your case.
  • For 6mm thickness board, repeat cutting to 3 times.

Machine opening sequence

  1. 順次序由近至遠開三個電制
  2. check地下冷郤機有否着綠燈開着
  3. 開窗邊盡頭抽氣扇機
  4. 用usb線連𦁉電腦,對電腦來說laser cuter只是一個printer
  5. 從電腦下面抽屜拎取鎖匙櫃的鎖匙
  6. 用鎖匙開啟鎖匙櫃，拎取laser cutter安全鎖匙
  7. 用laser cutter安全鎖匙啟動機器

Machine printing sequence

  1. Open the lid
  2. Lower the platform first
  3. move the laser head away from the board
  4. Place in material at the top left corner
  5. Close the lid
  6. Press auto focus
  7. Print the file in Coral draw
  8. Press "Start/Stop" to start laser cutting
  9. During cutting, if need to stop. One might press the "pause" button and press again to resume. Or one might press the "start/Stop" button to terminate the cutting. Pressing "start/Stop" button again would start cutting the board from the beginning.
  10. after finishing, wait for 30 seconds for the smoke to be fully pumped out first before opening the lid
  11. Open the lid
  12. lower the platform
  13. move the laser head away from the board
  14. take out the materials
  15. close the lid

Machine closing sequence

Resever all the step in opening sequence

  1. 用laser cutter安全鎖匙關閉機器
  2. 歸還laser cutter安全鎖匙至鎖匙櫃
  3. 關閉並鎖住鎖匙櫃
  4. 歸還鎖匙櫃鎖匙至電腦下面抽屜
  5. 移除連𦁉電腦的usb線
  6. 關掉窗邊盡頭抽氣扇機
  7. 順次序由遠至近關掉三個電制
  8. 地下冷郤機燈應該關着
