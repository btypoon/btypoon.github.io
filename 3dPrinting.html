Printing machineries

Flashforge Creator Pro x5

Flashforge Creator 3 x1

Filament Type

  • PLA (ecofriendly) 200 50 recommend 210 55
  • PETG (tougher) nozzle:210-250 plate:80-100 Recommend 240,90
  • ABS (toxic)

Calibration

Level the plateform manually. Insert paper and adjust three screws in the back Feel the small friction when pulling the paper. Not to tight, not too loose.

Filament Loading

Use only the right nozzle. the left nozzles were removed

Slicing Software

  • FlashPrint 5
  • 0.6mm path width
  • 119% extrusion rate

Beware

  • See if temperature can reach the target value. Old part or malfunction might cause temperature drop. You might turn the printing speed down to compensate the temperature drop of the nozzle.
  • Preheat the nozzle before cleaning
  • Unscrew the cover and clear the residue if some got stuck inside
  • Renew the filament if it was brittle
  • Watch out for tangled Filament at the nozzle when printing
  • Watch out for the filament coilling up in the hanger
