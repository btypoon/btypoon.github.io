Definition of Spin Glass

According to wiki, spin glass defines a disordered magnetic state of a material. Comparing to ferromagnetic materials, where their atomic magnetic spins all align in the same direction, spin glass material possess random magnetic spins and couplings too

The word "glass" was used as analogy between the atomic magnetic disorder property of spin glass to the atomic positional disorder property of conventional glasses.

In conventional glass, their atomic bond structures are highly irregular and amorphous. Materials exhibiting similar properties with no observed long-range periodicity are called amorphous, non-crystalline and sometime also described as "glassy".
